# Known Limitations

1. No Godot executable was available, so engine import, runtime smoke testing, in-engine test execution, manual playthrough, and export verification remain unperformed.
2. Level 2 platform spacing, ricochet angles, Warden charge timing, projectile reflect timing, gate duration, shockwave clearance, and Resonance Strike timing were designed from the current movement constants but still require interactive balancing.
3. Final artwork and audio are absent. Procedural fallbacks keep the level readable, but final asset scale and alignment must be reviewed after import.
4. Windows and Web export presets are present, but export templates were unavailable and no builds were generated.

No runtime-error count or engine-parser-error count is claimed without executing Godot.
