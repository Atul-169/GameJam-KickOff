# Gameplay Flow

## Full game

Main Menu → Automatic Prologue Cinematic → **Kick the Ancient Seal** → Hall of Still Gears → **Archive of Echoes — The Waking Warden** → Court of Guardians → The Sealed Heart → Collapse Escape → Ending → Credits.

Existing flow outside Level 2 is preserved.

## Level 2 — The Waking Warden

1. **FROZEN inspection**
   - Read the archive inscription.
   - Inspect the dormant Warden, Orb, runes, wall, platforms, Echo Lock, and gate.

2. **First Orb kick / KICKOFF**
   - Orb physics and archive gameplay activate.
   - Warden remains dormant until the first correct rune.

3. **EAR — Hearing**
   - Solve the first aimed ricochet.
   - Warden begins charging toward remembered sound locations instead of continuously tracking Arin.
   - Lure a charge into the cracked wall.

4. **Upper route**
   - Climb the newly opened platform route.
   - Reposition at the elevated Orb pedestal.

5. **EYE — Vision**
   - Complete the harder elevated ricochet.
   - Warden gains a vision cone, pursuit, and telegraphed echo projectiles.

6. **Echo Lock / timed gate**
   - Reflect a Warden projectile into the lock.
   - The gate opens for approximately 4.2 seconds.
   - Aim and kick the Orb through the opening.

7. **MOUTH — Voice**
   - Warden gains a scream telegraph and outward ground shockwave.
   - Jump the wave.
   - The Echo Core opens briefly after each scream.

8. **Resonance core loop**
   - Hold and release charged kick.
   - A delayed cyan Resonance Strike appears at the original kick-impact area.
   - Only an actual strike/core overlap during an exposure window counts.
   - One exposure accepts one hit; three unique hits defeat the Warden.

9. **Completion**
   - Dangerous temporary attacks are cleaned up.
   - Archive exit opens.
   - Existing Niko transmission plays.
   - Exit grants ECHO exactly once through the existing level-completion system.
   - Results continue to Level 3.

## Wrong rune

Wrong order resets the visible rune sequence and Orb position, deals one damage, and enrages the existing Warden. Its highest awakened ability stage remains active, so mistakes do not make the encounter easier.
